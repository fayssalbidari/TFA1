Late is an application designed to help you check if your train is running late, based on data provided by the SNCB. Interactive and fun, it offers precise information to help travelers plan their journeys more effectively. By optimizing your time and reducing the stress of the unexpected, Late transforms the experience of train travel in Belgium. Discover a new way to travel serenely and efficiently. With Late, every journey becomes more predictable and enjoyable, allowing you to make the most of your time.
